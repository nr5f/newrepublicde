Honor Medals

Basically all armies give out medals to honor their best soldiers. Basically it's up to the members which medals are placed on their uniforms. Sure characters can just add 5th fleet medals on their uniform they already earned, but since it's possible to add medals earned in other clubs and medals earned in Star Wars games to the uniforms as well.

Medals can be added in two different ways. On ceremonial or class A uniforms the complete medals are added. On Dress or class B uniforms a medal patch is added with square icons for ach medal in the colors of the medal ribbons. Medals are worn on the right side of the uniform, in case of uniforms that have a rank emblem on the right side of the uniform medals are placed directly above it.

 Republican Medals

The Republican Honor Medals is the basical set of medals that are given out in the 5th fleet.

 Imperial Medals Medals

Imperial Medals have been given out in the in the first two years when the 5th fleet was Imperial. These medals aren't given out anymore but a couple of 5th fleet members that earned medals in these times are still in the fleet.

 Game Medals

There are the medals of the following games:
X-Wing
TIE Fighter
X-Wing Alliance
Dark Forces

It's up to the members which of the medals they have earned are displayed on the uniform. An officer that has earned a Medal of Communication 3 times can add the 3 medals to his uniform, he can also add just one medal to his uniform or even noone. He can also add a single Medal of Communication to his uniform with a medal chip that represents 3 awards. With seven Medal of Communications he has even more possibilieties cause he can replace 5 of them with a silver Medal of Comunication.

Medals Chips

Medal Chips are small colored disks that are placed on the right side of the medal ribbons. The chips are a semi official way to display multiple awarded medals in the Republican navy. They are no official standart in the Republic, but most parts of the navy already use them. The Disks have the colors Black, Brown, Violet, Green, Blue, Red, Copper, Silver, Gold and Diamond representing the values one to ten. Soldiers that awarded more then medals of a kind may add multiple disks. One Diamond disk for each full contigent of 10 medals and colored disks for the remnant. Soldiers that got a medal a single time don'T add chips to it, not eaven a black disk. Black Disks are only used to display the values 11, 21, 31 and so on.
