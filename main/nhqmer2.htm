Game Medals

X-Wing Medals:

Officers can add the medals of the five original battles X-Wing to their Uniform, if they want to. Here is a small table with the medal ribbons.

TIE Fighter Medals:

Officers can add the medals of the thirteen original battles TIE Fighter to their Uniform, if they want to. Here is a small table with the medal ribbons.

X-Wing Alliance Medals:

Officers can add the medals of the original battles X-Wing Alliance to their Uniform, if they want to. Here is a small table with the medal ribbons.

You can also download the full medals for all the three games here: nhqgamem.zip

Dark Forces Medal:

In the game Dark Forces it was possible to get the Star of Alderaan at the end of the campaign. The medal is identical to the Star of Alderaan that is given in the game X-Wing so offices can use the X-Wing version for their uniform.
