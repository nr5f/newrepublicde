Uniform Database

Welcome, New Republican Officer, this is the uniform department and your uniforms will be given out here. There are three different unform that are worn, the Field Uniform, the Dress Uniform and the Ceremonial Uniform.
The Field Uniform is the uniform worn while normal duties. There is no great restriction on this uniform, so there are great differences from unit to unit. Battlegroup I for example completely uses the grey Imperial tunic. One thing all field uniforms have in common, there are no merits worn on field uniforms.
The traditional uniform of the 5th is the Dress Uniform. It's the green tunic of the former Rebel Alliance, you may remember it from X-Wing and XWA. The style may varie a bit from officer to officer, but the entire fleet uses this Dress Uniform.
Finally the Ceremonial Uniform, different to the Dress Uniform that uses medal ribbons, the Ceremonial one has full medals.
