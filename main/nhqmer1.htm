Republican Medals

General Honor Medals

(From highest to lowest. Has to be given to an officer by: FC, GC, CC, WC (Only FC for the Star of Alderaan))


Star of Alderaan: Highest 5th fleet Merit Medal. Only given for extremly heroic behavior against the enemies of the New Republic.

Republician Service Medal: Elite general honor medal of the 5th fleet. Given for extraordinary duties within the New Republican navy.

Golden Cycle: Advanced general honor medal of the 5th fleet. Given for excellent duties within the New Republican navy.

Blue Medallion: Basic general honor medal of the 5th fleet. Given for good duties within the New Republican navy.



Special Honor Medals

(Has to be given to an officer by: FC, GC, CC, WC)


Republican Suport Award: Given for getting 4 more members for the 5th fleet.

Golden Tournament Medal: Given for the 1st ranking in official tournaments.

Silver Tournament Medal: Given for the 2nd ranking in official tournaments.

Bronce Tournament Medal: Given for the 3rd ranking in official tournaments.

Carrier Captain Award: Carrier Captains can award this medal once per medal term to an officer on his ship that shows an outstanding performance. An officer can only get one Carrier Captain Award or one Wing Commander Award per medal term.

Original Design by Vice Admiral Martin Gustavson

Wing Commander Award: Wing Commanders can award this medal once per medal term to a pilot in his wing that shows an outstanding performance. An officer can only get one Carrier Captain Award or one Wing Commander Award per medal term.

Original Design by Vice Admiral Martin Gustavson

Leadership Award: For officers who played an important role in a tournament with another club.

Original Design by Comodore Rad Greystone

Training Medal: Awarded for an exellent graduation at the Naval Academy.

Advanced Training Medal: Awarded for solving the advanced naval academy exam.

Crimson Award for News and Information: For Officers who did great deads in the News and Information service.

Original Design by Comodore Darthboy

Hero of the Ground Forces: For Officers with extraordinary behaviour in the games Dark Forces, Jedi Knight, Jedi Outcast, Jedai Academy, Republic Commando and Battlefront 1 and 2. The Medal will be given by the Fleet Commander.

Hero of the Pilot Corps: For Officers with extraordinary behaviour in the games X-Wing, TIE Fighter, X-Wing versus TIE Fighter and X-Wing Alliance. The Medal will be given by the Fleet Commander.

Circle of Hope: Given to Officers done a good service in campaigns.

Original Design by High Admiral Commskywalker

Cross of Corellia: Given to Officers done a excellent service in campaigns.

Original Design by High Admiral Commskywalker

Courage of Solo: Given to Officers done a heroic service in campaigns.

Original Design by High Admiral Commskywalker

Valor of Ackbar: Given to Officers done a extraordinary service in campaigns.

Original Design by High Admiral Commskywalker

Morale Award: Given to Officers done a morale support to the fleet.

Original Design by High Admiral Commskywalker

Ladder Champion: The Ladder Champion Medal is given to highest listed ladder players of each gaming platform at the end of a ladder term.

Original Design by Admiral Martin Gustavson

Order of Ood Bnar: The Order of Ood Bnar is the highest medal within the Order of Jedi. It can only be given out once each half year by a Group Commander or higher.

Original Design by Admiral Martin Gustavson

Progress Coin: Given for superior RPG participation.

Star of Valor: The Star of Valor is the highest medal for RPG gaming. It can only be given out once each half year by a Group Commander or higher.

Jedi Honor Medal: The Jedi Honor Medal is given to members of he OoJ for good duties, only Jedi Council members can give out this medal.

Original Design by Commodore Rene Andries

Hero of the Tactical Command: For Officers with extraordinary behaviour in the games Rebellion, Force Commander, Galactic Battleground and Empire at War. The Medal will be given by the Fleet Commander.

Battle Medals

(Has to be given to an officer by: FC, GC, CC, WC, SC)


Medal of Communication: Given to officers showing much activity on fleet IRC channels, fleet message boards and other forms of interent communication.

Original Design by Admiral Martin Gustavson

Silver Medal of Communication: The Silver Medal of Communication isn't given to officers, but Officers who already awarded 5 Medals of Communication, can replace them with a silver one.

Original Design by Admiral Martin Gustavson

Golden Medal of Communication: The Golden Medal of Communication isn't given to officers, but Officers who already awarded 5 Silver Medals of Communication, can replace them with a golden one.

Original Design by Admiral Martin Gustavson

Blue Star: Battle Medallion for the 1st 5th fleet TIE Fighter battle.

Medal of Battle: Given to Officers which shown their skills in battle.

Original Design by High Admiral Commskywalker

Good Performance Award: Given to Officers which shown good performance in RPGs or Run Ons.
