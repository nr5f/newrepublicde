Old Imperial Medals

In the years 1998 and 1999 the 5th fleet was an Imperial fleet and gave out several Imperial medals. Officers who earned some of these medals usually still wear them on their uniforms.

General Honor Medals

Emperor's Will: Highest Imperial Merit Medal. Only given to heros of the empire by the Naval High Command.

golden Imperial Cross of Efficiency: Given for extraordinary performance in the Imperial navy.

golden Imperial Cross of Loyality: Given for extraordinary loyality in fights for the Imperial navy.

golden Imperial Cross of Courage: Given for extraordinary courage in fights for the Imperial navy.

silver Imperial Cross of Efficiency: Given for good performance in the Imperial navy.

silver Imperial Cross of Loyality: Given for shown loyality in fights for the Imperial navy.

silver Imperial Cross of Courage: Given for shown courage in fights for the Imperial navy.



Special Honor Medals

Amber Fighter Medal: Given for superior flight-performance.

Crimson Fighter Medal: Given for good flight-performance.

Cyan Fighter Medal: Given for more then normal flight-performance.

Imperial Ground Forces Medal: Given for superior duty in an ground combat.

Imperial Service Cross: Given for good duty in an ground combat.

Bavarian Battle Medal: Given for supreme tactical decisions in a strategy game.

Merit of Development: Given for extraordinary performance in developing.

Imperial Security Bureau Medal: Awarded for extraordinary performance for the Imperial Security Bureau.

Medal of Artifice: Awarded for supreme tactical decisions in a roleplaying game.

Medal of Information: Awarded for sucessful spying in a roleplaying game.

Awards of Sabotage: Awarded for sabotaging the enemy in a roleplaying game.

Awards of Infiltrating: Awarded for infiltrating the enemy in a roleplaying game.

Training Medal: Given for extraordinary trainings performance.



Special Issue Medals

(Has to be given to an officer by: FC. Had been given out at a special issue)


Havok Campaign Medal: The Award is given to those Officers fighting in the Havok Campaign.

Sec Campaign Medal: The Award is given to those Officers fighting in the Sec Campaign.



Battle Medals

(Has to be given to an officer by: FC, GC, CC, WC, SC)


Medal of Concord: Given to officers for successfully completing NHQ-Battle 1. (Invasion on Aleria III)

Tella's Tear: Given to officers for successfully completing NHQ-Battle 2. (The end of the 5th fleet?)

Red Kalidor: Given to officers for successfully completing NHQ-Battle 3. (The Prometheus)

Imperial Battle Medal Step 1: Given to officers for successfully completing NHQ-Battle 4. (Bio Crisis)

Imperial Battle Medal Step 2: Given to officers for successfully completing NHQ-Battle 5. (Unknown Dangers)

Phantom Medal: Given to officers for successfully completing NHQ-Battle 6. (Stealth Technologie)

Interceptor Medal: Given to officers for successfully completing NHQ-Battle 7. (Rebels and Hapans)

Honor of Tekkas: Given to officers for successfully completing NHQ-Battle 8. (Battle at Tekkas)
