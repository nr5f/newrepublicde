Career Progress

Well, especially officers in the ranks of Ensign and Second Lieutenant should check here:
http://www.mindblock.net/nrforum/viewtopic.php?t=696
The Page contains a list of requirements Ensigns and Second Lieutenants need to proceed one rank.
There requirements should be very easy to complete. Just contact me as soon as you reached them cause of your promotion, in case you didn't already got promoted automatically.

A second good possibility is recruiting. Recruiters often get instantan promotions. Depending to the number of recruits you may even get fast promotions in middle and higher ranks. Just make sure Commodore Mattias Gustafsson and me person know which officers are recruited by you.

The 3rd possibility is creating your own ship. Requirement is that you bring 3 additional members into the fleet that play the crew of the ship. We currently try to fill the actual ships so it don't makes sense creating a new ship without even a small crew. But on the other hand administrating a ship is a difficult task so it's a good way to proceed fast through junior and middle ranks.

19th August 2005: New Courses at the Academy

http://www.newrepublic.de/academy/courses.htm

There are new courses at the academy, the Advanced Naval Academy exam has been splitten into 3 different courses: An officers grade, a commander's grade and a captain's grade. Officers can get an Advanced Training Medal for each of the courses. So everyone may get 3 ATMs in total, exept of those who managed the old Advanced Academy Exam. They can get 3 ATM aditionally to the one they already got.

You can directly start doing the couses, but please wait till Tax is back for sending in the results. You can also help each other with the exams, just please don't post answers to the questions on the message board or in the main channel or so.

I think the captain's grade is pretty difficult, or at least should be, sore for an officer that is one year or more in the fleet it should be no problem, but for a junior officer, well we will see.
