Positions 

Line Fleet Command positions

Fleet Commander (FC):
(Minimum Ranking of Admiral [AD])
The Fleet Commander can promote officers up to the ranking of an Admiral [AD]. He also can give every medal, except for the Emperor's Will, to his officers and soldiers. The Fleet Commander anounces all the Group Commanders as well as the Special Officers and the Executive Officer of the fleet

Group Commander (GC):
(Minimum Ranking of Vice Admiral [VA])
A group is a part of a regular fleet, but it can also be a smaller independent unit of ships. A fleet group has usually the strength of 2 to 4 Stardestroyers with escorts, a carrier group has usually the strengh of 1 Stardestroyer with escorts a fleet has normally the strengh of 10 to 25 Stardestroyers with escorts. The Group Commander can promote officers up to the ranking of an Admiral [AD]. He also can give every medal, except for the Emperor's Will, to his officers and soldiers. The Group Commander anounces his Executive Officer.

Executive Officer (XO):
(Minimum Ranking of Rear Admiral [RA])
The Executive Officer is the assistant of a fleet commander or a group commander. He can promote officers to the ranking of a Captain [CPT], and he is allowed to give every medal to them except for the Emperor's Will.

Special Officers

Trainings Officer (TRO):
(Minimum Ranking of Comodore [CMD])
The Trainings Officer has to guide the new recruits through the system of the 5th fleet. That means he has to answer the recruit's questions, tell the recruit what's up in the 5th and how the recruit has to behave in the 5th. He may promote cadets to Ensign [EN] before graduation. He promotes his cadets after they've finished their training. He can give the Trainings Medal. He can award the following medals:
-Medal of Development
-Imperial Cross of Efficiency (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Courage (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Loyality (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Efficiency (gold)
-Imperial Cross of Courage (gold)
-Imperial Cross of Loyality (gold)

Recruiting Officer (RCO):
(Minimum Ranking of Comodore [CMD])
The Recruiting Officer has to get new members for the 5th. He has to make the advertising for the fleet in chats and the internet. He can award the following medals:

-Imperial Cross of Efficiency (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Courage (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Loyality (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Efficiency (gold)
-Imperial Cross of Courage (gold)
-Imperial Cross of Loyality (gold)

Tactical Officer (TAC):
(Minimum Ranking of Comodore [CMD])

Ship Command Positions

Carrier Captain (CC):
(Minimum Ranking of Captain [CPT])
The Carrier Captain commands his carrier and reports to the Fleet High command. He can promote officers up to the ranking of Captain [CPT], and he is also allowed to give the following merrits to them:
-Cyan Fighter Medal
-Crimson Fighter Medal
-Amber fighter Medal
-Imperial Service Cross
-Imperial Ground Forces Medal
-Imperial Cross of Efficiency (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Courage (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Loyality (silver)
The CC has to make the the ship page, if the High Command orders.

Division Commander (DC):
(Minimum Ranking Commander [CM])
The Division Commander commands his Division consisting of several platoons and he has to report to the CC. He can promote officers up to the ranking of a Captain [CPT]. He is allowed to give the following merrits to the soldiers of his division.
-Imperial Service Cross
-Imperial Ground Forces Medal

Wing Commander (WC):
(Minimum Ranking Commander [CM])
The Wing Commander commands six squadrons and reports to the CC. He can promote officers up to the ranking of Captain [CPT], and he is also allowed to give the following merrits to them:
-Cyan Fighter Medal
-Crimson Fighter Medal
-Amber fighter Medal

Command Support Positions

Head Assistant (HA):
(Minimum Ranking of a First Lieutenant [LT])
The Head Assistant has the assist the special officer he has been asigned too and he has to coordinate the work of his Assistants. Every special office can have one Head Assistant. The Head Assistant can give the following medals:
-Medal of Development
-Imperial Cross of Efficiency (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Courage (silver)
-Imperial Cross of Loyality (silver)

Assistant (A):
(Minimum ranking of Corporal [CRP])
The Assistant has the assist the special officer he has been asigned too. Every special office can have three Assistants.

Adjutant (ADJ):
(Minimum ranking of Sergeant [SGT])
The Adjutant has the assist the officer he has been asigned too. Fleet Commander and each Group Commander can have one Adjutant.

Squadron Commander (SC):
(Minimum Ranking of a First Lieutenant [LT])
The Squadron Commander has to command the squadron and to report to the Wing Commander every two weeks. He decides, who will become FL, FM and VSC in his squadron. If he thinks, an officer earned a promotion or a medal, he has to report it to the wing commander or to the Carrier Captain if there is no Wing Commander. He is also allowed to give battle medals to members of his squadron. These are the medals that will be awarded to pilots when they completed a NHQ battle on hard. The Squadron Commander usually has the duty to make the homepage for his squadron, VSC and higher officers are allowed to support the SC with the page.

Vice Squadron Commander (VSC):
(Minimum Ranking of a Second Lieutenat [LT])
The Vice Squadron Commander's duty is the same as the Flight member's, but he also has to assist the Squadron Commander. The SC decides in which way.

Flight Leader (FL):
(Minimum Ranking of a Ensign [EN])
The Flight Leader's duties are the same as the Flight Member's except for one: he has to hold contact with his Flight members.

Flight Member (FL):
(Minimum Ranking of a Ensign [EN])
The Flight Member's duty is to try to complete some of the NHQ Battles as well as stay active. So he has to send a report to the SC all two weeks in elite squadrons every week. If a squadron member isn't abel to respond, he has to inform the Squadron commander. If he doesn't inform the squadron commander within two weeks, he will get one warning. This warning is an e-mail which must be responded within one week. Flight members who don't response this warning will be taken off the roster. He is also allowed to make his biography and his uniform.

This warning is an e-mail which must be responded within two week. Platoon members who don't response this warning will be taken off the roster. He is also allowed to make his biography and his uniform.

Security Classifications 

Imperial Personal Identification
Everyone in the Imperial forces has an ID so he is easyer to identify. The ID usually has the following structure:
Ranking/Position Name/Asigned garrison, ship, station, .../Innerstructural Asignment

Security Classes
Almost every Imperial information with military contents is classified. An Imperial officer or soldier needs a security permission of the same grade as the security class of the document to see it. He is allowed to see oll information with the same or lower security classes even if the information is absolutly unnnessesary for him. If an officer has a lower security grade than the docment, he need a permission of someone, who has the same security class as the document or a higher.

Class 5 "Civillian I"
Civillians without special comission usually have class 5.

Class 4 "Civillian II"
Military personal without ranking have class 4.

Class 3 "Soldier"
A soldier who is not part of a special unit (like the SOSF Units) has Class 3 in most casses. This Class is divided into 4 sub-classes (3a+, 3a, 3b, 3c). For Example 3a is the same as 2c, only 2c is for officers and 3a for soldiers.

Class 2 "Officer"
Officers usually get class 2. Class 2 is divided into 4 sub-classes familiar to Class 3. (2a+, 2a, 2b, 2c) 2a is equal to 1c.

Class 1 "Commander"
Officers with high rankings (like General or Rear Admiral) or commanders of huge units get Class 1. Class 1 is divided into 3 sub-classes. (1a, 1b, 1c) The Council of the Grand Admirals has 1a.
 
Class 0 "Agent"
Class 0 is divided into 3 subclasses. (0a, 0b, 0c) 0c is dedicated for members of the Secret Order command staff and for special infiltrators of the Secret Council. 0b is for the head of the Secret Order and for members of the Secret Council. 0a is for the head of the Secret Council and for the Emperor's Hands.
