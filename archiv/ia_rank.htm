Promotion Guideline
The following table explaines, when an officer is able to reach the next higher ranking.

Cadet

Time Requirements to get promoted to: none
Special Requirements to get promoted to: Registring

Ensign

TR: none
SR: Extraordinary duty as Cadet

skipped

skipped

Second Lieutenant

TR: none
SR: Graduation as Cadet

First Lieutenant

TR: none
SR: Graduation as Ensign

TR: Minimum of 2 weeks being Second Lieutenant
SR: Extraordinary normal duties, Special duties, asigning as Squadron Commander or higher or 1.5 month active Second Lieutenant

Lieutenant Commander

TR: Minimum of 2 weeks being First Lieutenant
SR: Extraordinary normal duties, Special duties or 2 month active First Lieutenant

Commander

TR: Minimum of 3 weeks being Lieutenant Commander
SR: Extraordinary normal duties, Special duties, asigning as Wing Commander or higher or 3 month active Lieutenant Commander

Senior Commander

TR: Minimum of 3 weeks being Commander
SR: Extraordinary normal duties or Special duties

Captain

TR: Minimum of 3 weeks being Senior Commander
SR: Extraordinary normal duties, Special duties or asigning as Carrier Captain or higher

Line Captain

TR: Minimum of 3 weeks being Captain
SR: Extraordinary normal duties, Special duties or 3 month active Captain

usually skipped

Commodore *

TR: Minimum of 2 weeks being Line Captain
SR: Extraordinary normal duties or Special duties
Leadership and Loyality has to be proved

TR: Minimum of 1 month being Captain
SR: Extraordinary normal duties or Special duties
Leadership and Loyality has to be proved

Rear Admiral *

TR: Minimum of 1 month being Commodore
SR: Extraordinary normal duties or Special duties
Leadership and Loyality has to be proved

Vice Admiral *

TR: Minimum of 1.5 month being Rear Admiral
SR: Extraordinary normal duties or Special duties
Leadership and Loyality has to be proved

Admiral *

TR: Minimum of 2 month being Vice Admiral
SR: Extraordinary normal duties or Special duties
Leadership and Loyality has to be proved

Fleet Admiral *
(Will be anonced by the 1st Naval Director or higher)

TR: Minimum of 3 month being Admiral
SR: Special duties or Leadership duties
Superior Leadership and Importance to the existance of the Fleet have to be proved. A Fleet Admiral must have let a Battlegroup for some time

High Admiral *
(Will be anonced by the 1st Naval Director or higher)

TR: Minimum of 3 month being Fleet Admiral
SR: Special duties or Leadership duties
Superior Leadership and Importance to the existance of the Fleet have to be proved. A Fleet Admiral must have let a Battlegroup for some time

Grand Admiral *
(Will be anonced by the 1st Naval Director or higher)

TR: Minimum of 3 month being HA or 3 month being Grand Admiral of a lower grade (In case of the promotion to a higher grade Grand Admiral)
SR: Special duties or Leadership duties
Superior Leadership and Importance to the existance of the Fleet have to be proved. A Fleet Admiral must have let a Battlegroup for some time

* Officers with the ranking of Comodore to Admiral can request a half step, if they have their ranking more than 4 month, Officers with the ranking of Fleet Admiral to Grand Admiral can request a half step if they have theit ranking more than 5 month. If an Officer from Comodore to Grand Admiral gets a halfstep, this is not seen as promotion, only as upgrading.
