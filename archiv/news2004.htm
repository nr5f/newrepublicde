20th June 2004: Celestrial Fury (update)

Frontline Report:

Fondor fallen to the Star Vipers
The last Republican bastion in the region fallen to the Star Vipers. After the remnants of Battlegroup I left the region for the repair docks of Sluis Van. The New Republican Harrendor fleet and the Fondor system defense got encycled by Star Vipers forces and got wiped out. With the lost of Fondor the Star Vipers finally fortified their dominance on the sectors.

Rebel Squadrons used wormhole to Sullust
The Rebel Squadrons used a wormhole to replace the weakened 5th fleet unit, that tried to defend Sullust against the Star Vipers. The suprize of the incomming Republican fleet from the wormhole forced the Star Vipers to withdraw from Sullust.

ISD Immortal sneaks behind enemy lines
While Star Vipers controlled Khomm was under serious attack by several fractions, Rear Admiral Rene Andries managed to sneak behind the lines of the Star Vipers with his Carrier Group and managed to destroy the starfortress and the supply depots. Parts of Battlegroup Hunter started an attack on the system then and the Vipers had to withdraw. The 5th Fleet controls the system now.

9th June 2004: Celestrial Fury (update)

Frontline Report:

Recon Y-Wings just returned from Honoghr reporting no sign of survivors found.
Exept of derbis from former space platforms and ships no sign of life has been found on Honoghr. Exept of a few smaller ships of the Dark Brotherhood moving through the sector. The attacking fleet of the Brotherhood seems to have disappeared. Every ship that didn't manage to escape with the first wave seems have been destroyed. Tomorrow the Republic holds a minute of silence for the 24th infantry devision that got lost on Honoghr. Survivors of the first wave are still on the way home.

Sluis Van secured.
Parts of Battlegroup Zentar moved to Sluis Van last week. High Admiral Commskywalker reports Sluis Van is now under full control of the Republic.

Heavy battles between Star Vipers and 5th Fleet near Umgul.
4 Days ago the Starvipers defeated Battlegroup I at Umgul as well as the Rebel Squadrons at Kalar'ba. Now they control the triangle Yag'Dhul - Umgul - Kalar'ba and start heavy attack from the against the nearby Republican systems. The remaining parts of the 5th fleet in this area have been summoned to defend the systems and push the Star Vipers off the triangle.

VSD Ratio lost a Ungul.
The VSD Ratio, the oldest ship of the 5th fleet, holded the flank of Battlegroup I when they were starting an assault against the incomming Star Vipers fleet at Umgul. Under Massive Bombing the Reactor Core of the Ratio overheated. Not many of the VSD Ratios personnel managed to escape the explosion. When the Ratio got destroyed the Star Vipers tried to encycle Battlegroup I without success, but the Fighter defense of Battlegroup I had not eough fighters to prevent the Star Vipers effectiv from making torpedoruns against the Republican Battleships. The remnants of Battlegroup I managed to retreat from Umgul, but with the loss of the Ratio, the reducing of the fighter squadrons and the hull damage on several battleships Battlegroup I lost around 50% strength.

Grand Admiral Michael Simon

2nd May 2004: High Command Meeting Results

Well, the basic point of the meeting was to remove some negative developments. Several ships which are inactive have been removed. Officers have been concentrated on the active ships to strenghthen theese units and decisions are made that ships don't fall into inactivity anymore.

To the decisions in detail, The following ships have been removed:
VSD Ratio
CRS Hellraiser
FRG Dark Sky
DC Zevildos
The ships stay for RPG issues untill the got replaced with active ships again.

Vice Admiral Rechtien, Lieutenant Commander Lanwermeyer and Lieutenant Commander Samoray from the VSD Ratio get transferred to the ISD Hamburg XII.
Vice Admiral Jedi3, Captain Harm Brandai, Commander Darji and Lieutenant Commander Neosophite from the CRS Hellraiser get transferred to the BB Zentar.
Captain Master-Qui-Gon from the CRS Hellraiser gets transferred to the CRS Payback.
Commodore Fliegenpilz and Commander Radix from the FRG Dark Sky get transferred to the ISD Hamburg XII.
Admiral Martin Gustavsson from the DC Zevildos gets transferred to the ASD Prometheus.
Captain Seth Xaxik additionally gets transferred to the BB Zentar

Officers can apply for getting their own (single squadron) ship now, but they need to recruit a crew for that ship in a specified amount of time now, decided by the Group Commander. Ships can be removed if the get in active. Activity of ships are regulary checked by the Group Commander. In case a ship is more the 50% filled with really active officers a ship can also be upgraded to a bigger type of ship.

Hopefully this will increase the cooperation within a ship.

6th April 2004: Preparing for Battle

Coruscant:
3 hours ago the Republican senate had a non-public meeting concerning the increasing of Imperial remnant activities all over the galaxy. In the last weeks the activities had increased drastically till the climax 2 days ago, when a heavily guarded Republican convoi got attacked. The Republic lost 76 freighters and 7 escort ships. The famous CRS Ambassador was one of the losses. The emergency meeting today shows, the Republic takes this issues serious.

Annette Falkner
Galactic News Central (GNC)

Delasol:
Many things happened after the council meeting last week. The speaker of the senate said, that the Republican military forces are at alert, but the Republic wouldn't take actions and still observes the situation. But on Delaso and other important fleet bases of the New Republic observation seems to be something different then we thought it would be. The 5th Republican fleet as well as sector and planetary defense forces are summoning here. The government reactivated one Imperium Class Stardestroyer, two Victory Class Stardestroyers and 52 smaller warships as well as more then 200000 veterans from the civil war. 5 entire divisions have been loaded into troop transporters and the streets of all mayor cities on delasol are still filled with streams of soldiers waiting for shuttles to be brought to one of the endless number of ships that darken the Skies.
