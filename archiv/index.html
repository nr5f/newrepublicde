History of the 5th Fleet

Between Clone Wars and the Battle of Endor

The Old Republic is in war with an alliance of Seperatists and their Droid Armies and builds a Clone Army to defeat them. Close to the end of the Conflict Chancellor Palpetine forms the Republic into the 1st Galactic Empire and turns against the Jedi Order which was former part of the Old Republic. While the Empire defeats the last Droid Armies a Rebellion forms against the Empire. Several years after the birth of te Empire the Rebel Alliance manages to destroy the Imperial Death Star with the help of the Jedi Luke Skywalker in the Battle of Yarvin. Four years later the growing Alliance manages to destroy the second Death Star with Emperor Palpetine onboard as well in the Battle of Endor and starts the downgoing of the Galactic Empire.

The 5th fleet was a basic Republican fleet going through the military development of the Clone wars and the following Imperial era. At the beginning of the Clone wars almoste every ship of the fleet got replaced through Acclamators, which got replaced through Victory and Venator Class Stardestroyers during the end phase of the war and the following years. After fighting against the seperatists the 5th fleet got asigned to enhance the Imperial control in the core regions, later the rim sections. Before the battle of endor the 5th fleet consisted of 6 battlegroups with 5 Stardestroyers in each; 23 Stardestroyers of the Imperial Class, 7 Stardestroyers of the Victory Class.


The Battle of Endor and the following years

With the loss of the emperor and the other Imperial leaders the head of the empire degenerated and the Rebels started to form the New Republic. In the follwing years the Remnants of the empire tried to get the galaxy back, but just experinced an almost endless number of defeats.

Pre 1998
In the years after the Battle of Endor the 5th fleet lost several ships in battles against the Rebellion.
Fleet Admiral Michael Simon became Fleet Commander of the 5th regular fleet and transferred a couple of his personnel from the 11th fleet to the 5th fleet. In the first time when Michael had the command the fleet suffered the problems they had before, that the war was already lost on several fronts and withup supplies and reinforcements he had to withdraw the remnants of the fleet to more secured areas.

5 years past Endor

Grand Admiral Thrawn had taken over the command of the major remaining Imperial forces. In a galaxy-wide campaign he managed to push the Republic back to the core regions. Thrawn's fragile plan ended when his own Noghri bodyguard killed him and Captain Palleon ordered the retreat of the remaining forces.
The Grand Admiral had build up his progress to fragile so the Remnant wasn't able to profit from the progress after Thrawn was dead.

1998
Back in Imperial controlled territories the new Grand Admiral Michael Simon began to meet with the other local Imperial Commanders to discuss the future of the empire. Many of the higher officers had the oppinions the old structures weren't working anymore after the top leaders were dead for so long. The different Imperial commanders weren't working together anymore, in some reagions Imperial commanders had started fighting against each other. When Michael got a position in the navy command he tried to get the Imperials together again, and he also started the Eclypse Class Superstardestroyer Dominant project. Grand Admiral Frank Andries became temporary Fleet Commander in that period.
The 5th fleet had formed up with the 1st fleet under Admiral Vacula and the 3rd fleet under High Admiral Jjerrows when the internal differences between the Imperial factions had reached the Delasol where Michael was stationed.

6 years past Endor

Emperor Palpatine had been reborn in a clone body and takes Luke Skywalker as apprentice. The emperor failed to take control over the galaxy again and lost his flagship the Eclipse and the galactic weapon.

1999
Michael was meanwhile commander of the navy had brought several Imperial leaders on his side but negotions got more and more difficult. The 5th fleet started to get more and more into conflicts with other Imperial fractions, many officers started having problems with Michael's way of command, especially Michael's Imperial command tone which became harder while the fleet was growing, there were also a couple of higher navy officers seeking for more power within the navy. Michael had brought large fleets together when the fleet started to destableize, and before before they were able to play an Important role in the war between remnants and the alliance. In a campaign against an opponent Imperial warlord Michael lost the loyality of great parts of the fleets. After long fights Michael and the few standing loyal to him withdrawn to Delasol and fortified there.
It took a while but isolted from the otther Imperial fractions the remainders of the 5th and their allies around Delasol decided to join the Republic. The 3rd fleet and their allies continued to attack te 5th fleet, but Michael avoided an open battle till the 3rd fleet concentrated on other things. The 5th fleet started to recove, reduced to one and a half battlegroup and short time lateer Battlegroup Zentar got formed, first under the command of Admiral Martin Gustavsson, later led by Admiral Commskywalker.

The restructured fleet got soon inflicted in some border skirmishes with battlegroups of Grand Admiral Larsen's armada. In this time the 5th fleet had negotiations with Fleet Admiral Worf. When the Fleet Admiral decided to join the Republic battlegroup Hunter got formed out of parts of Imperial veterans from the 5th and Worf's fleet.
The '1st Outer Rim War' broke between large groups of Republican and Imperial units. The 5th fleet joined the war almost 2 month after it had broken out to reinforce the Republican forces in Cadrel. After the arrival at Cadrel the 5th fleet encountered Grand Admiral Larsen's units again.

7 years past Endor

After Mon Mothma had retired, Princess Leia Organo-Solo suceeded her and became chief of state of the New Republic.
The remaining Deathstar prototype got destroyed in the Maw cluster by Kyp Dyrron.

2001
Republic and Remnants had lost a lot of fighters, especially in the last weeks of the war. The elite pilots of the empire were able to take out most of the republican fighter squadrons. But they Imperials weren't able to break the Republican infantries dominance over the planets itself and Republican commandos forces the Imperial fleets to retreat by taking out their support stations and freighters. The Imperial fleet was forced to return to Elornia, a system they captured earlier in the conflict.

2002
The 5th fleet and other Republican forces had been replaced by other Republican contingents in the Cadrel area and were stationed at Nerid'or. A small Imperial task force tried to take out the Republican forces there while they were unprepared in the 'Battle at Nerid'or' but the Republicans managed to defend against the Imperial attackers.
The '2nd Outer Rim War' followed shortly. The 5th fleet and the other Republican forces launshed a heavy assault against the Imperial fortifications at Elornia. After the Imperials lost the heavy fights at Elornia the Republic invaded the neighboring Binaural sector. The Imperials had summoned their forces too slowly so the Republic were able to conquer the sector.
The second Outer Rim War were the last conflict where the 5th fleet was confronted with Grand Admiral Larsen's forces. The 5th fleet lost the track of the Remnant warlord after the conflict.

8 years past Endor

Durga had hired Bevel Lemelisk to create a new superweapon for him, the Darksaber, a ship with the super-laser-cannon of the Deathstar. With the help of General Crix Madine a Republican task force managed to destroy the ship through chasing it into an asteroid field.
Admiral Daala assasinates several leading Imperial commanders and makes herself commander of their remaining forces. With a fleet led by her new flagship, the Superstardestroyer Knight Hammer, she attacked the Jedi Academy at Yavin IV, but after the destruction of the ship the surviving Remnant forces fled from Yavin 4 and Admiral Daala retired from her command and Vice Admiral Palleon followed as commander.

2003
In the 'Ambush at Consollenor' the 5th fleet attacked Remnant forces at Consollenor and uncovered plans of Warlord Horus.
In the following 'Remants of the Past' Campaign Several Remnant forces attacked Rebel installations supported by Warlord Horus.

2007
The 5th fleet started an offensive against the Backyard of Warlord Horus territories called 'Hunting the Ghost' Campaign. After causing large casaulties to Horus' fleets the fleet returned home for refitting.
Short after returning home the '3rd Outer Rim War' began. Admiral Ackbar sends the 5th fleet in the Outer Rim Regions again to stop the progress of Sith and Imperial forces in the Region. The Republican forces got pushed back to the Azemin system by Sith forces that had earlier ended the Remnant Dominance in the sector. But while the Republic expected the last assault from the Sith, the Sith fleets got removed from the sector cause of unknown resons.
