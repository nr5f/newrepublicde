ISD Hamburg XII

The Hamburg XII is an Imperium Class Stardestroyer that left the shipyard a few years past Endor. The Hamburg XII replaced the Hamburg XI in the 5th fleet that got lost during the civil war. Most systems have been upgraded meanwhile so the ship is equal to an ISD of the 4th generation.
