ASD Prometheus

The ASD ship class was concepted in the late phase of the Galactic Civil War and the prototype, the Prometheus got constructed short after he Battle of Endor. It has been the flagship of the 11th fleet for some years till it replaced the dominant that got lost when the 5th turned to the New Republic.
