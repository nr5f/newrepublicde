Carrier Captain (CC)

Carrier Captain ASD Prometheus
Minimum Ranking: Commondore
The ASD Prometheus is a pretty uncommon ship for the 5th fleet. Many traditions of this ship come from the original 5th, so squadrons, squadron names and squadron equipment can't be changed by Carrier Captain or Wing Commander. Also the minimum requirement for getting pilot on the Prometheus is Lieutenant.
An officer tha applies for this position should be very active. He should be in Battlegroup I or should at least be ex-Imperial
Uniforms on the ASD Prometheus are grey Imperial tunics.
Someone that knows a way to get additional personnel for the Prometheus is preferred.
Contact Grand Admiral Michael Simon.

Command Assistant (CA)

Command Assistant Recruiting Office
Minimum Ranking: Ensign
The position in the Recruiting Office Requires an Officer that has some talent in recruiting new members. Alternativly he could have experiance in advetising a club or webpage in the internet.
Command Assistant in the recruiting office is also a good chance to get some higher ranks faster cause officers in that position can get bonus promotions for a number of recruits they made that graduated.
An officer that knows a good way to get a couple of new members could leave his squadrons for some weeks or months to get some recruits and return to his squadron then a few ranks higher.
Contact Grand Admiral Michael Simon.

Adjutant (ADJ)

Adjutant in several battlegroups
Minimum Ranking: Lieutenant Commander
Adjutant is a positon in the same level as Vice Squadron Commander, but just has roleplaying meaning. As Adjutant you can't be in a squadron. Adjutants are asigned on the flagship of the specific battlegroups.
In RPGs and Run Ons the adjutant often transmits orders from the Group Commander and often takes part on Fleet Command meetings where the Group Commander takes part too.
Currently all the battlegroups lack of an adjutant, if you have interest in such a postion contact your group commander.

Deck Officer (DO)

Deck Officer several ships
Minimum Ranking: Lieutenant
Deck Officer is a positon in the same level as Flight Leader, but just has roleplaying meaning. As Deck Officer you can't be in a squadron.
In RPGs and Run Ons Deck officers are usually the officers on a bridge of a carrier, navigations officer, communications officer and so on.
You should contact your Carrier Captain for such a position.
