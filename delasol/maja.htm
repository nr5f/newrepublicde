Maja System

The Maja System consists of a small blue sun "Maja", two planets "Central Maja" and "Praetorian" and a huge astroid belt.

Central Maja is a small sized red colored planet without athmosphere. Three Imperial garrisons are located deep inside this huge rock to protect thousants of IPBMs stationed here. The Republic overtaken this garrisons but given it up after a short time. The IPBMs have been transported away for recycling. Only the biospheres and the old tunnels of the garrisons remained on the planet.

Praetorian is a huge dshungel world with several ancient temples. Serveral Republican repair platforms are stationed in the orbit of this world. Praetorian is on of the most important Republican space docks in the entire sector. On the planet itself is just a small research base. The docks, ship yards and the repair platforms are all small space stations, which are lightly defended. The planets strongest defense is the Luebeck Starfortress, the last of 3 Imperial superstructures that guarded the planet before.

The empire build several mining facilities in the asteroid belt to raise enough minerals for the production on Praetorian, now the New Republic is owning this facilities.
