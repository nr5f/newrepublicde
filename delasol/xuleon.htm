Xuleon System

Xuleon is a red star with two planets. Neteremonis is the most inner planet of the Xuleon system. It's a huge farm world producing the food for 1/3 of the Imperial troops around. It's heavyly guarded by several platforms orbiting the planet, but most of the stations in the orbit are docks for freighters and storage tanks. The single moon of Neteremonis is an entire big Imperial garrison. The New Reublican high command beleaves there are more than 500 starfighters and more than 200000 troops stationed. The second planet of Xuleon is Partaknonum, a gas giant with 7 moons. The 5th of the moons, Pleniak, has several mining stations and the 6th moon, Epondared, is a small fleet base for the system defense forces.
