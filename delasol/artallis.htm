Ar' Tallis System

Ar' Tallis is a small system with a red sun, Ar'Tallis, and two small rocky planets, Klotz and Brocken. Brocken is the bigger one of both planets and has a Nitrogen atmosphere while Klotz has no atmosphere. The distance between Klotz and Ar' Tallis is between 1 and 1.4 AE; the distance between Brocken and Ar' Tallis is about 1.7 AE. The Ice-Comet BE17005 also belongs to the system.

The New Republic has a star fortress called Barad Anuar orbiting Klotz and two smaller Platforms Anuar Denfense I amd Anuar Defense II in a similar orbit. There are also a few civillian stations orbiting Klotz.

Barad Anuar is one of the biggest star fortresses the New Republic captured from the empire. More then 200000 civilians living in the fortress plus another 25000 military personal. 5 years past Endor the fortress became the Republican capitol of the sector and still is. Just a few Republican covettes are protecting the system, cause the fortress itself is pretty strong itself, te last Imperial officer that tried to recapture the fortress was Rear Admiral Holkawan, but he lost an entire Victory Class Stardestroyer as well as a few corvettes on the attempt.
