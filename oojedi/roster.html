Jedi Padawan

To get a padawan, a Jedi Initiate needs to be accepted by a Jedi Knight or a Jedi Master as his student. A Jedi Knight or Jedi Master can just have one padawan student at the same time. Once elected, a padawan can stay padawan even without having a master, but he cannot become a Jedi Knight until he has a new master that completes his training.
