The Order of Jedi does not only consist of Master Luke Skywalker's Jedi Academy. Far away from Yavin IV a few members of the Order formed houses and clans.
 

The Jedi Academy

The Jedi Academy, placed in the former hideout of the rebellion against the empire on the moon of Yavin IV. The Jedi Academy is not only a place for padawans, the best masters of the force are there to train their students in the force. The Jedi Academy is lead by Jedi Master Commskywalker. Everyone in the Order of Jedi is part of the temple in case he isn't member of a house.
 

The Houses

Houses are small groups within the Order of Jedi. Each of the houses follows the Jedi Codex, the difference between the houses and the temple is more a point of style and tradition. Members of the Order can just belong to one of the houses at the same time.

House Andries

House Andries has a dark history. The house is consisting of the family and friends of the family. Many of it's members were dark Jedis or officers in the empire before.
