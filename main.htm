Project S
Well Project S cause it is following after Project P. I will focus Project P as well later in the report. At the moment it's just important that Project P is over and Project S has started.

Especially now, where we have reached a timeframe of the star wars universe where the Imperial remnant isn't the thread for the New Republic anymore it was before.

Also discussions about Top S4 can be placed here as well.

The main points of Project R:

Well, how does it work. Well, you send me a private message with character name, callsign and e-mail adress of the officer you recruited (and maybe more optional information like preferred avatar/skills/reached ranks in other clubs/star wars games he owns).
Would be good sending a copy to Matt Sifi as well. I will reply pins and temporary passwords.
The recruit will bypass the academy and directly get ensign in your unit.

Depending on how many recruits you bring and how active they initially are you will your own unit, filled with your new officers. Maybe 3 or 4 a squadron, 7,8 or 9 an own wing command, higher numbers an own ship command.

Besides this there are some personal awards for officers:
- You will earn a Blue Medallion for the first active officer you recruit for the fleet in this project.
- You will earn a Golden Cycle for the second active officer you recruit for the fleet in this project.
- You will earn a Republican Service Medal for each four officers you recruit for the fleet in this project.
- You will get an advantage of 2 weeks on your next promotion for each offices you recruit for the fleet in this project.

Minimum time between two promotions is one week. So if you are ensign and recruit someone, your promotion will be stored for the promotion to lieutenant, cause the required time for Second Lieutenant is just one week.
So the ensign would get promoted to Second Lieutenant after one week, and after that he would get his advantage and get promoted to Lieutenant after one week.
A high number of recruits can also be a reason for a promotion to Fleet Admiral, High Admiral.

You can also negotiate with leaders of other Clubs, Fleets, Communities, Gaming Clans or however these groups are called these days to join the 5th or merge with us. If you can convince anoth smaller club to join us each member of the joining club counts as recruit for the officers responsible for the negotiations.
Remember don't recruit from other clubs, negotiations with the leaders of other clubs are ok, contacting their members without the knowledge of their leaders isn't ok.
