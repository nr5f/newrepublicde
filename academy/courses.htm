Naval Academy Exam

The Naval Academy Exam is nessesary to become officer in the New Republic. There are 17 easy questions about Starwars and the 5th fleet manual, where you can get a maximum of 100 points. 51 points are nessesary to graduate, 85 points are necessary to graduate with a Training Medal.
If a cadet misses the required 51 points he can redo the test as often as he wants to till he manages to get enough points to graduate. After graduation he gets asigned to a squadron on one of the 5th fleets battleships.
It's usefull to read the Fleet Manual before you do the naval exam or better open it in a second window to have it as reference while you're solving the questions.

Naval Academy Officer's Exam

You can get a total of 100 points, 60 points are nessesary to pass the exam and get an Advanced Training Medal. The exam is about the duties of an officer in the 5th fleet.
If an officers failes the exam, he can redo it as often as he wants to till he passes the exam.
It's usefull to read the Fleet Manual before you do the naval exam or better open it in a second window to have it as reference while you're solving the questions. The exam requires a minimum of knowledge in editing of graphics, if you are new to the topic ask one of the more experianced 5th fleet officers to help you.
