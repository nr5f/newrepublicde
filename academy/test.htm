Naval Academy Exam

(Version 1.4)

Welcome to the graduation test of the Naval Academy on Coruscant. In the following test 100 points are possible. 51 points are nessesary to graduate, 85 are nessesary to graduate with a Training Medal. Its even possible to solve the test if you haven't seen at least one Star Wars movie, but knowledge of the Star Wars plot can be helpfull. You can try the test as often as you want to and if you have any questions, feel free to ask any officer in the 5th fleet. The answers for the questions 9 and following can be found in the Fleet Manual. It's usefull to open the Fleet Manual in another window as a reference while you're doing the test. When you have done the test you can send the answers to High Admiral Matt Sifi (mattsias@gmail.com).
